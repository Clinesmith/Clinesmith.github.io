Fort Worth through the Eyes of Data

I don’t want to move to Fort Worth after college, but I want to want to move to Fort Worth after college. The job market is extremely appealing for someone with my interest in embedded software, I could live near downtown without breaking the bank, and, given the Fort Worth is something of a little brother to Dallas, I’d be within 45 minutes of all of Dallas’ attractions without having to deal with the same traffic when I just want to spend a quiet night closer to home. Ultimately, though, I didn’t find any especially compelling reasons why I should make my way down to the Lone Star State after I graduate.
